## Modern UI/UX Guidelines (GSAP & Animations)
Filament v5 natively integrates with modern animation libraries.
- For GSAP integrations in Livewire v4, use `x-init` or `wire:init` hooks to trigger animations on component mount.
- Prefer Tailwind v4's built-in animation utilities for simple transitions.
